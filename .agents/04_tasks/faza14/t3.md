## 3. TEHNICKI ZAHTJEVI

### 3.1 MQTT topic struktura za senzore
- `hotel/{id}/room/{num}/temperature` — payload: `{ "value": 24.5, "unit": "celsius" }`
- `hotel/{id}/room/{num}/occupancy` — payload: `{ "present": true, "confidence": 95 }`
- `hotel/{id}/room/{num}/window` — payload: `{ "state": "open" | "closed", "position": 50 }`

### 3.2 SensorService
- `SensorService` — subscribe na sve senzor topice
- Tabela `sensor_readings`:
  - `id`, `room_id`, `sensor_type` (TEMPERATURE, OCCUPANCY, WINDOW), `value` (JSON), `timestamp`
  - Retention: 30 dana (archiviraj starije)
- API:
  - `GET /api/smart-rooms/{roomId}/sensors/current` — trenutna očitanja
  - `GET /api/smart-rooms/{roomId}/sensors/history?type=temperature&from=...&to=...` — historija

### 3.3 HVAC automacija na osnovu prisutnosti
- Ako `occupancy.present == false` duže od 30 minuta:
  - Postavi temperaturu na ECO režim (18°C zima / 28°C ljeto)
- Ako `occupancy.present == true`:
  - Vrati na komfornu temperaturu (22°C)
- Komanda se šalje putem MQTT: `hotel/{id}/room/{num}/hvac` → `{ "command": "set_temp", "value": 18 }`

### 3.4 Alarmi
- Temperatura ispod 5°C (opasnost od smrzavanja) → alert
- Temperatura iznad 40°C (požar) → hitni alert
- Senzor ne šalje podatke 60 minuta → "sensor offline" notifikacija
