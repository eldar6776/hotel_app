## 3. TEHNICKI ZAHTJEVI

### 3.1 Email servis
```
IEmailService
  SendConfirmation(bookingId: Guid): Task<SendResult>
  SendCancellation(bookingId: Guid): Task<SendResult>

EmailConfiguration
  - SmtpHost (string)
  - SmtpPort (int)
  - SmtpUsername (string)
  - SmtpPassword (string — enkriptovano u bazi)
  - FromAddress (string)
  - FromName (string)
  - UseTls (bool)
```

### 3.2 Email template
- Lokacija: `HotelPro.Infrastructure/Email/Templates/BookingConfirmation.html`
- Placeholderi: `{{GuestName}}`, `{{HotelName}}`, `{{RoomType}}`, `{{Arrival}}`, `{{Departure}}`, `{{Nights}}`, `{{TotalPrice}}`, `{{BookingNumber}}`, `{{Status}}`
- Fallback plain-text verzija ako HTML nije podrzan
- Template je staticki fajl (embedded resource), ne cuva se u bazi

### 3.3 Automatsko slanje
- Trigger: nakon POST `/api/bookings` kada je status = Confirmed
- Async slanje (fire-and-forget sa retry mehanizmom)
- Logovanje uspjesnosti slanja u tabelu `EmailLog`:
  - BookingId, Recipient, SentAt, Status (Sent/Failed), ErrorMessage
- Retry: 3 pokusaja, razmak 5 minuta, nakon toga logovati kao Failed

### 3.4 SMTP konfiguracija
- Citati iz appsettings.json ili iz baze (tabela `AppSettings`)
- Admin panel za konfiguraciju (dodatni task, ne u ovom)
- Inicijalna konfiguracija kroz `appsettings.json`
